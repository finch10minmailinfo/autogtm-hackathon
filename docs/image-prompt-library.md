## 2. LEGO product photography — Nano Banana
Create a professional product photography image of a **[PRODUCT]** reimagined
entirely in LEGO bricks. The design should:

**Product Design**
- Construct the iconic shape/form using LEGO bricks and elements in brand-appropriate colors
- Include the official brand logo prominently displayed using pixelated LEGO mosaic style
- Maintain 100% recognizability while being clearly made of LEGO pieces
- Show detailed brick construction with visible studs and connector pieces
- Make the product appear fully functional and three-dimensional

**Visual Style**
- High-end product photography with studio lighting
- Professional depth of field with soft focus background
- Rich, vibrant colors that match the brand identity
- Add floating elements or particles around the product that match brand colors
- Dramatic studio lighting with soft shadows creating dimension
- Reflective surfaces showing product quality
- 3D rendered or AI-generated photorealistic style

**Composition**
- Centered product as the focal point
- 4:5 aspect ratio (vertical orientation)
- Background color that complements the brand’s color palette
- Product positioned to showcase all key details
- Professional studio setting with gradient or solid colored background

**Brand Integration**
- Official brand logo visible prominently (or as part of the LEGO construction)
- Color scheme derived from the brand’s identity
- Lighting and atmosphere that reflects the brand’s premium positioning

Style: Professional product photography, high-quality 3D rendering, premium studio lighting
